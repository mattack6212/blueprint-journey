Your Monthly Commitment

The Most Important Input from Here

You just deployed the system. It's running. But the single most important thing that happens from this moment forward is not something you just did — it's something you keep doing.

Your Monthly Commitment.

Your Monthly Commitment is the dollar amount that flows into your system every month on the same date. It is the fuel. The system cannot compound what it does not receive.

Consistency Beats Amount

$200 every month for 24 months will build more than $2,000 twice and then nothing for a year. The system rewards the steward who shows up — not the one who tries hardest for a short stretch.

You do not need to commit a large amount. You need to commit a consistent amount. Pick a number you can flow in every single month without fail — through busy months, tight months, and quiet months. That is your Monthly Commitment.

Set It. Don't Pause It.

Your recurring transfer runs automatically. Once it is set, do not interrupt it. Do not pause it when things feel uncertain. Do not reduce it to "catch up" on something else. Treat it like a bill — non-negotiable, on the calendar, done.

If you need to adjust it, adjust it up when you can. But never pause it down to zero. Even $50 flowing in every month beats the month a steward skipped because life got noisy.

"Whoever can be trusted with very little can also be trusted with much." — Luke 16:10

The system cannot compound what it does not receive. Your Monthly Commitment is the one decision you make once and then keep honoring. Everything else runs on its own.
