Phase Two

Route

The second layer of The Blueprint.

Earn builds your portfolio. Route uses your portfolio to absorb your monthly expenses — routing your real-life cash flow through the system you've built.

Route is more powerful than Earn. It's also more demanding. It assumes you've run Earn long enough to understand how your portfolio behaves through normal months and rough months.

You'll know you're ready when

  • You've completed The Blueprint Journey
  • You've run Earn for at least 3–6 months
  • You're comfortable with your monthly rhythm
  • You're considering how to use your portfolio for more than dividend reinvestment
